Hake fillet

Perfect brunch meal

This is a perfect dish for a morning brunch before kicking the day

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 avocado
  • 2 slices of bread
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Fry the eggs
  2. Toast the slices of bread
  3. Peal, chop and mash the avocado
  4. Put one egg on each slice of bread and half of the mashed avocado
