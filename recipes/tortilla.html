Tortilla

The delicious typical tortilla from Spain

This dish is made with potatoes, eggs and onions. You basically chop the potatoes and onions, stir the eggs and cook everything in a pan. This is a typical dish from spain but loved all around the world. The best tortilla is the one that my grandmother cooks.

Ingredients

  • 2 potatoes
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 onion
  • Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut the potatoes and the onions
  2. Stir the eggs in a bowl
  3. Set the pan in low fire
  4. Put the onion in the pan
  5. Once the onion is half-way cooked, put the potatoe in the pan
  6. Wait untill everything is almost cooked (about 15 minutes)
  7. Pour the stirred eggs in the pan covering everything and wait for about 5 minutes
  8. Take a plate, put it over the pan and quickly flip so the tortilla falls in it, then put back the other way round
  9. Wait for about 5 minutes untill that side is cooked
  10. Take the plate again and repeat but this time don't put it back and voila!
